Master Recipe

This recipe is the most common recipe in the world that every chef must be know!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato
  • Oil
  • Lettuce

Instructions

  1. knead the dough into a thin rectangle
  2. spread the tomato over the dough
  3. put oil over the tomato
  4. bake up to 100 degrees for 5 minutes
  5. serve with lettuce
